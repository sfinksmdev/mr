Privacy Policy and Terms

In this page we inform the visitors about the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when using MR. By installing our application you agree with the following police regarding your personal information.

What information does MR collect?

MR requires the permission of accessing the camera of the device and the microphone in order to collect their data only at the moment of being recorded. These permissions are essential for the purpose of MR.

How MR uses the information?

When MR is collecting information from the camera and/or microphone from the device, which is always under consentment of the user, MR stores all the collected information in the MR folder located on the main storage of the device. MR always places the collected information on the local device.

What information does MR share?

None. All the recorded media is never shared or accessed by MR after its creation.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Updates to this privacy policy will be made with new releases of MR. It is advised to check this page frequently to check new changes. Changes of this Privacy Policy are effective immediately and will be notified on this page.

Additional Information

The information presented on this page is only related with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when using MR. For other topics check the description of the app on Google Play, the information displayed in the app, or contact us by email.
